About me

I'm currently working at BMD software, which is a company that provides computational solutions for better decision making and knowledge management in health and life science industries. My main field of expertise is the development and innovation of of medical imaging solutions (e.g. PACS), data integration between different information systems, and also data analysis/mining over big data repositories.

My PhD thesis was entitled as "A federated architecture for biomedical data integration". I did the PhD in (MAPi program), attended classes at FEUP (Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto) and I was enroled and working at Universidade de Aveiro, IEETA.

In the past, I worked with Network Security and network adminstration in Umit Project and I'm a very intusiastic for Open Source projects. I did a master degree in Univeristy of Aveiro in Master of Science on Computer and Telematics Engineering (2006 - 2011).

As a researcher, I've been working with healthcare records and medical imaging. I started working on Dicoogle project since 2008. Since then I've been working in indexing data from medical repository, share medical image in distributed enviroments and cloud Computing.
